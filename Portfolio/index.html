Diabetic Retinopathy Detection


Early detection using CNN to prevent vision loss.


In this project, introduce the diabetic retinopathy perception exploiting the deep learning approaches. This will be very useful to the people in order to save their time. The significant objective of the project is to achieve the creation of easily transportable, inexpensive way to detect the DR by capturing the images of the eye of the individual because many of us were affected by this by taking later or improper treatments. This design will lead to way for the capable of analysing and recognising the images to stick out the results within the few minutes. It will predict the disease within the few minutes after the input of the retinal fundus image by doing all the processes. This system is optimized well to detect the DR more efficiently by using the CNN algorithm. As the result it will give the condition or stage of the DR.

Smart Ration System


Improves transparency in ration distribution.


The current ration system contains lacking transparency and efficiency, often forces beneficiaries to make repeated visits, wasting both time and effort, while ration shop employees struggle to manage the crowd.Our proposed solution — a smart web-based application for slot booking, real-time stock tracking, and viewing shop timings — provides a practical and scalable solution to these issues. By allowing beneficiaries to schedule their visits at their convenience, check the availability of products, and avoid unnecessary trips, thus this website significantly reduces waiting times.

Interactive Mobile Application for Small Scale Organic Farming


Supports farmers with guidance and knowledge.


In this project, gives precise, easy-to-understand organic farming steps and videos, tutorial contents, techniques in fertilizer preparations. Its front end, backend, and storage choices use React.js for a clean running and seamless scaling experience with Node.js at its back-end and MongoDB at the storage section. One of the highlights of the app is the inclusion of a Convolutional Neural Network (CNN) model, which allows farmers to upload photos of crops and make instant predictions regarding possible plant diseases, as well as recommended cures.
